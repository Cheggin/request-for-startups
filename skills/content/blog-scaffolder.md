## Description

Content agent creates SEO-optimized blog posts for the startup. Topics are derived from the product spec and competitor research. Posts are published to the website and linked from the landing page.

## Checklist

- [ ] Blog section added to the Next.js website (e.g., /blog)
- [ ] MDX or markdown-based blog post system with frontmatter
- [ ] Topic generation from product spec keywords and competitor gaps
- [ ] SEO optimization — target keyword, meta description, heading structure
- [ ] Internal linking to product pages and other blog posts
- [ ] Blog post index page with pagination
- [ ] Blog linked from landing page navigation
- [ ] RSS feed generated for blog posts
- [ ] Blog posts included in sitemap.xml
- [ ] Open Graph images generated per post
- [ ] Publishing cadence — at least one post per week
- [ ] Content quality check — readability, grammar, factual accuracy

## Notes

- Blog topics should target long-tail keywords relevant to the startup's product category
- Competitor research from the product spec can reveal content gaps to fill
- Blog posts double as social media content — the social-media-agent can promote them
