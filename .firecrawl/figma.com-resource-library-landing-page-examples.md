Getting people onto your website is one thing. Getting them to take action—sign up, buy, register—is another. That’s where effective landing pages come in.

With the right design, a landing page guides visitors toward a clear goal—and increases conversions. A focused layout, minimal distractions, and visuals aligned with user intent can also help lower your cost per lead.

Read on to learn:

- 16 landing page examples and customization ideas
- Landing page best practices

## [**Example 1: Product launch landing page**](https://www.figma.com/resource-library/landing-page-examples/\#example-1-product-launch-landing-page)

![ A screenshot of Figma’s modern product launch landing page template](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/599r6htc/regionalized/06435b6a55da2c8c508b4505fa5a560acedc5c00-1920x1080.png?w=1920&h=1080&q=75&fit=max&auto=format)

A successful product launch generates excitement, [connects with users](https://www.figma.com/blog/creating-moments-of-connection/), and provides clarity to potential customers. This [modern product launch template](https://www.figma.com/community/file/1487309170684591074/modern-product-launch) is designed for maximum impact, leading with a compelling headline and a strong hero visual to instantly communicate the product’s core value.

The product landing page design is clean with plenty of white space. ensuring feature highlights and pricing tiers are easy to digest. The “Discover More” button lets visitors explore the product before committing.

**Tip for motivating customer action:** Use social proof, such as quotes from beta testers, to build instant trust and credibility.

**Make it yours:** Update the color palette and [typography](https://www.figma.com/resource-library/typography-in-design/) to reflect your brand. Swap in high-fidelity product mockups or an animated GIF to show the product in use.

## [**Example 2: Gym landing page**](https://www.figma.com/resource-library/landing-page-examples/\#example-2-gym-landing-page)

![A screenshot of Figma’s performance-driven gym landing page template](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/599r6htc/regionalized/00007d6a81e69c23ba5a0e21b4766f8091b7384c-1920x1080.png?w=1920&h=1080&q=75&fit=max&auto=format)

Bold imagery and results-oriented language help this [performance-driven template](https://www.figma.com/community/file/1498113978492175844/performance-driven-gym) connect with people motivated by fitness goals. It includes space for class schedules, testimonials, and facility features, helping reduce hesitation and encourage sign-ups.

The layout emphasizes professional yet energetic visuals, giving visitors confidence that they can achieve their desired outcomes. It also emphasizes community and encourages viewers to join.

**Tip to motivate customer action:** Offer an easy entry point, like a free seven-day pass or a complimentary personal training session. Capture leads by clearly highlighting the transformation a customer can expect, not just the features of the gym.

**Make it yours:** Use real photos of your facility and trainers to establish a local, personal connection. Add a simple tool to check class availability by day or location.

## [**Example 3: Legal service landing page**](https://www.figma.com/resource-library/landing-page-examples/\#example-3-legal-service-landing-page)

![A screenshot of Figma’s sincere professional legal services landing page template](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/599r6htc/regionalized/1db56e0e3e42fc8d9476cf833979abdb7baf683e-1920x1080.png?w=1920&h=1080&q=75&fit=max&auto=format)

Trust and professionalism are essential for anyone seeking legal help. This [professional legal services template](https://www.figma.com/community/file/1498100241451260378/sincere-professional-legal-services) uses a muted color scheme and a structured layout to convey reliability and expertise.

Dedicated sections highlight practice areas, lawyer credentials, and client quotes. The design prioritizes ease of contact, ensuring potential clients can quickly book a confidential consultation.

**Tip to motivate customer action:** Use professional headshots and clear bios of your legal team to humanize the service. Add accreditation logos and a detailed privacy policy link near the consultation form to reassure visitors about confidentiality.

**Make it yours:** Customize the practice area descriptions to match your specialization, whether that’s corporate law, family law, or estate planning. Insert quotes from past clients who’ve been satisfied with your services.

## [**Example 4: Financial services landing page**](https://www.figma.com/resource-library/landing-page-examples/\#example-4-financial-services-landing-page)

![A screenshot of Figma’s friendly accounting services landing page template](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/599r6htc/regionalized/80dd092c01948d5b6d70b41215af7f9c8b6df703-1920x1080.png?w=1920&h=1080&q=75&fit=max&auto=format)

Managing money can be intimidating, so this [friendly accounting services template](https://www.figma.com/community/file/1498084903136440786/friendly-accounting-services) keeps things approachable, clear, and trustworthy. It simplifies complex topics through clean layouts, friendly typography, and plain-language copy.

Key [web design elements](https://www.figma.com/resource-library/what-is-web-design/) make it easy to outline the client onboarding process, show transparent pricing, and include testimonials to establish credibility and confidence. The goal is to establish your services as a supportive partner, not just a spreadsheet processor.

**Tip to motivate customer action:** Add an interactive quiz or a simple calculator to help visitors understand which services they need, while also capturing leads.

**Make it yours:** Tailor the messaging to your audience, like freelancers or small businesses, and use data-driven visuals to highlight outcomes you’ve delivered.

## [**Example 5: E-commerce landing page**](https://www.figma.com/resource-library/landing-page-examples/\#example-5-e-commerce-landing-page)

![A screenshot of Figma’s personal collector’s items landing page template](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/599r6htc/regionalized/0eaa706781eda2d861cb7c2801993afb063aaa99-1920x1080.png?w=1920&h=1080&q=75&fit=max&auto=format)

E-commerce sites for [personal collectors’ items](https://www.figma.com/community/file/1498117463617016293/personal-collectors-items) should prioritize product showcases and narratives. ## [**Landing page best practices**](https://www.figma.com/resource-library/landing-page-examples/\#landing-page-best-practices)

A landing page’s impact comes down to clear strategy, not just looks. Whether you’re using a Figma template or building from scratch, following these best practices can help ensure your page converts.

- **Simple navigation.** Minimize or remove top-level navigation. The goal is to keep users focused on a single outcome—not send them elsewhere.
- **Compelling CTAs.** Make sure your call-to-action buttons are visually prominent. Use contrasting colors, and employ action-oriented language (“Start Your Free Trial,” “Get a Quote,” “Enroll Now”). Ideally, a primary CTA should be above the fold.
- **Trust signals.** Use elements like security badges, customer testimonials, star ratings, company [logos](https://www.figma.com/resource-library/types-of-logos/), or privacy policy links to build credibility and reduce hesitation..
- **Match intent.** Align your page copy with the ad or link that brought users in. Consistent messaging keeps bounce rates low and trust high.
- **Clear visual hierarchy.** Use design elements like size, color, spacing, and font weight to guide the visitor’s eye through the page in a logical order, from the main value proposition to the supporting features. The most important information (headline and CTA) should be the most visually dominant elements.
