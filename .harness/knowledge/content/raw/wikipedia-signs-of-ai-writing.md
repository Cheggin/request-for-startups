# Signs of AI Writing (Wikipedia)

Comprehensive catalog of AI writing tells documented by Wikipedia editors.

## Key Categories:
- Content-level: promotional language, inflated significance, superficial analysis
- Language: banned words (delve, tapestry, testament), copula avoidance, rule of three
- Style: title case, excessive bold, em dash overuse, inline-header bullets
- Tone: press-release voice, collaborative language, vague attribution
- Technical: markdown artifacts, broken citations, placeholder text

## Most Documented AI Words (by era):
- 2023-mid-2024: delve, intricate, tapestry, testament, additionally, boasts
- Mid-2024-mid-2025: emphasizing, enhance, fostering, pivotal
- Mid-2025+: highlighting, showcasing, plus notability-focused terms

Source: NPR, TechCrunch, Fast Company all covered this Wikipedia guide as the definitive reference.
